Description

The Crash is a gripping psychological thriller by #1 New York Times bestselling author Freida McFadden. The story centers on Tegan, an eight-months-pregnant woman who, feeling isolated and eager to escape her deteriorating circumstances, decides to leave her past behind. However, the nightmare she's fleeing is nothing compared to the unforeseen dangers that lie ahead. This Barnes & Noble Exclusive Edition features an exclusive playlist curated to enhance the reading experience.
