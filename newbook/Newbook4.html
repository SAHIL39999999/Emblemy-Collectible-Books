Description

Quicksilver is a 2024 romantasy novel by American author Callie Hart. It is the first book in the Fae & Alchemy series. The story follows Saeris Fane, a 24-year-old skilled thief with mysterious alchemical powers. While surviving in a harsh desert land ruled by an oppressive queen, Saeris accidentally triggers a gateway to a Fae realm. There, she encounters Kingfisher, a handsome Fae warrior with secrets and nefarious agendas of his own. Bound together by fate, they must navigate a centuries-long conflict that threatens both their worlds. Quicksilver masterfully blends elements of fantasy, romance, and adventure, creating a richly imagined world filled with magic and conflict. The novel explores themes of identity, power, and the consequences of one's choices. With its compelling characters and intricate plot, Quicksilver has captivated readers and critics alike, becoming an instant #1 New York Times bestseller.
